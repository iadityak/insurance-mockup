Frequently Asked Questions

Layoff insurance, also known as job loss insurance or income protection, is a policy that provides financial support if you lose your job due to a layoff or retrenchment. It pays out a percentage of your salary for a fixed period, helping you cover expenses while you search for a new job.

Any salaried employee working full-time in a registered company in India is eligible to apply. Eligibility for specific plans may depend on your industry, salary, and job stability. We currently do not cover self-employed individuals or contract workers.

To process a claim, you will typically need to provide your official termination letter from your employer, your final payslips, and a copy of your bank statement showing your last salary credit. This helps us verify the layoff and your income details swiftly.

No, your CareerShield policy payout is independent of any severance package you may receive from your employer. Our goal is to provide an additional layer of financial security during your transition, regardless of your company's severance policy.
