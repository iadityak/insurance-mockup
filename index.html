Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is this?

It's a private financial product designed to provide you with a fixed monthly income if you experience an involuntary job loss. Think of it as a personal income safety net, completely independent of any employer-provided severance.

Who is this for?

This is for any salaried professional in India who wants to protect their income and lifestyle from the uncertainty of the job market. It's particularly valuable for those in dynamic industries like tech, startups, and services.

How does the 2-year waiting period work?

To ensure the long-term stability of the community fund, you must be a subscriber for a minimum of two full years before you are eligible to make a claim. This prevents immediate claims and builds a strong, sustainable capital reserve to protect all members.

How are claims verified?

Claims are verified digitally and securely. We require an official termination letter from your previous employer and use public records like ITR and EPFO to confirm your employment status. This ensures a fair and transparent process for everyone.
